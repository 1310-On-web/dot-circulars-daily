The Indian government's circular mandates that all officers of the ITS Group 'A' and TES Group 'B' must submit their Annual Immovable Property Returns (AIPR) for the year ending 2025 through the SPARROW portal by January 31, 2026. It emphasizes the importance of timely submission and warns that failure to comply may lead to disciplinary action. Additionally, it specifies that no physical copies of the returns will be accepted, and all submissions must be made online.
