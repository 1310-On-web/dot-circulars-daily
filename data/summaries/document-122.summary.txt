The circular from the Ministry of Communications announces the reversion of Shri Saurav Kumar Singh from his local officiating position in the Senior Time Scale (STS) of the Indian Telecommunications Service (ITS) Group 'A' back to his substantive grade as a Junior Time Scale (JTS) officer. This change is effective from the specified date due to his release for training at IIT Delhi. The circular also instructs that necessary charge reports be prepared and submitted by the relevant authorities.
