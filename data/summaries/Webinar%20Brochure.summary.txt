The Indian government's circular announces a webinar on "Advancements in Optical Fibre Technologies," scheduled for December 12, 2025. The event will feature discussions on Hollow Core Fibres and Multi-Core Fibres, with presentations from experts in the field, including Shri Deepak Jain and Shri Sudipta Bhaumik. The key purpose of the webinar is to share insights on the current status and future perspectives of these technologies, fostering knowledge exchange among professionals in telecommunications.
