The Indian government's circular invites sealed tenders for the sale of old newspapers, magazines, waste paper, and other plastic waste from the Department of Telecommunications for a one-year period. Bidders must submit their quotations by December 19, 2025, along with an earnest money deposit of Rs. 7,500, and the contract will be awarded based on the highest quoted rates. The successful bidder is responsible for the regular removal and management of the waste materials at their own cost.
